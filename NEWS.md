# socialchange 0.1.0

* Initial release.
* `decompose_events()`: event-based decomposition for discrete entry/exit populations.
* `decompose_aggregated()`: microsimulation-based decomposition from aggregated cross-sectional data.
* `sim_social_change()`: forward simulation of social change with full event-by-event records.
* `cr_ic()`: cohort replacement vs. intracohort change decompositions (AD, LD, AD+, Model).
* `apc()`: Age-Period-Cohort models using orthogonal polynomial contrasts.
