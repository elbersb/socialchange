# Changelog

## socialchange 0.1.0

- Initial release.
- [`decompose_events()`](https://elbersb.github.io/socialchange/reference/decompose_events.md):
  event-based decomposition for discrete entry/exit populations.
- [`decompose_aggregated()`](https://elbersb.github.io/socialchange/reference/decompose_aggregated.md):
  microsimulation-based decomposition from aggregated cross-sectional
  data.
- [`sim_social_change()`](https://elbersb.github.io/socialchange/reference/sim_social_change.md):
  forward simulation of social change with full event-by-event records.
- [`cr_ic()`](https://elbersb.github.io/socialchange/reference/cr_ic.md):
  cohort replacement vs. intracohort change decompositions (AD, LD, AD+,
  Model).
- [`apc()`](https://elbersb.github.io/socialchange/reference/apc.md):
  Age-Period-Cohort models using orthogonal polynomial contrasts.
